How can we prevent this?


Avoid clicking on suspicious links. Also, avoid forwarding chain messages and do not leave devices unattended, especially in public areas. Use a password manager like lastpass - Lastpass remembers which websites you visited and the passwords. If there is a lookalike fake website, (e.g facebo0k.com), it will not remember the password. If you think your password has been compromised, immediately change your password
