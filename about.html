About

Devil's Whisper is a Brainhack 2021 Project to raise awareness on adversarial (audio) attacks on Smartphones and Smarthome Assistants


Disclaimer

The adversarial (Hidden Audio) attack's success may depend on various factors, including (but not limited to)

Audio file type (wav, mp3)
Volume of audio
Speaker type and quality
Distance of speaker from target
Background noise
Target device type
Target device version
Sensitivity of microphone of target
Angle between speaker and target
